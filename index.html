🎮 Pass & Play

Mini games for one shared phone — gather round like the old days. 💛

No servers, no accounts, no “add friend”. Your phone is the board. Put it on the table, everyone sits around it, and you take turns on the same screen — just like a real board game. Works offline, nothing to install.
Pop in your names (saved on your phone) and the game cheers you on with desi commentary — “Kya baat {name}, maza aa gaya!” 🎙️🔥
