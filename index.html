🎮 Pass & Play

Mini games for one shared phone — gather round like the old days. 💛

Your phone is the board. Put it on the table, everyone sits around it, and you take turns on the same screen — just like a real board game. Works offline, nothing to install.
Pull up a chair, press play, and unwind — with soothing background music to set the mood. 🎶
